Resources

For those who want to go deeper. Books, documentaries, primary sources, and organizations — curated for accuracy and accessibility.

Essential Books

The Ethnic Cleansing of Palestine

Ilan Pappé (2006) — Oneworld Publications

A detailed account of Plan Dalet and the systematic expulsion of Palestinians in 1948, based on Israeli military archives and oral histories.

The Birth of the Palestinian Refugee Problem, 1947–1949

Benny Morris (1988) — Cambridge University Press

The foundational scholarly work on the Palestinian refugee crisis, written by an Israeli historian using declassified Israeli state archives. Revisited edition published in 2004.

The Iron Wall: Israel and the Arab World

Avi Shlaim (2001) — W.W. Norton

A comprehensive history of Israel's relationship with the Arab world, written by an Israeli-British historian and one of the "New Historians."

The Question of Palestine

Edward Said (1979) — Vintage Books

A foundational work by the Palestinian-American scholar, examining the Palestinian experience through the lens of postcolonial studies and international law.

Documentaries & Films

Al-Nakba: The Palestinian Catastrophe

Al Jazeera (2008) — 4-part documentary series

A comprehensive documentary tracing the history of Palestine from 1799 to 1948, featuring interviews with historians, survivors, and archival footage.

1948: Creation & Catastrophe

Ahlam Muhtaseb & Andy Trimlett (2017)

Told through the voices of both Palestinians and Israelis, this documentary provides a balanced account of the events of 1948.

The Gatekeepers

Dror Moreh (2012)

Interviews with all six surviving former heads of Shin Bet, Israel's internal security service, who speak candidly about the occupation and its consequences.

Organizations

B'Tselem

The Israeli Information Center for Human Rights in the Occupied Territories

Israel's leading human rights organization, documenting human rights violations in the occupied territories since 1989.

Institute for Palestine Studies

Established 1963, Beirut

The oldest independent research institute devoted to the study of Palestine, publishing the Journal of Palestine Studies since 1971.

UNRWA

United Nations Relief and Works Agency for Palestine Refugees

Established in 1949 to provide relief to Palestinian refugees. Still operating today, serving more than 5.9 million registered refugees.
